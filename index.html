Pig Game Rules
  1. The game is played between two players.
  2. The goal is to reach 100 points to win the game.
  3. On a player's turn, they can roll the dice or hold their score.
  4. Rolling the dice adds the number to the current score. Rolling a 1 resets the current score, and the turn switches to the other player.
  5. Holding the score adds the current score to the total score and ends the turn.
  6. The first player to reach or exceed 100 points wins the game.
  7. Click the "New Game" button at any time to start a fresh game.
